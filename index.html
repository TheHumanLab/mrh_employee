Purpose Statement

The MRH Operational Diagnostic is a structured assessment tool designed to gather ground-level insights from employees across our supply chain operations. This diagnostic captures your direct experience with daily processes, cross-functional collaboration, customer touchpoints, and operational challenges. Your honest feedback will help identify improvement opportunities, streamline workflows, and enhance overall operational excellence across the organization.
